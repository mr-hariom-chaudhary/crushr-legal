Child Safety Standards

Crushr is committed to maintaining a safe platform and protecting minors from exploitation and abuse.

🔞

Age Requirement

Crushr is a dating application strictly intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. Users are required to confirm their age during registration. Any account found to belong to a minor will be immediately terminated.

🚫

Zero Tolerance Policy

Crushr has a strict zero-tolerance policy against:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) of any kind
  • Content that sexualizes, exploits, or endangers minors
  • Grooming or solicitation of minors
  • Any communication designed to exploit children
Any user found violating these standards will be permanently banned and reported to the relevant authorities immediately.
🛡️

Prevention Measures

We take the following measures to prevent harm to minors:

  • Age verification during account registration
  • In-app reporting tools for all users to flag inappropriate content
  • Manual review of reported accounts and content
  • Immediate suspension of accounts under investigation
  • Cooperation with law enforcement when required
📢

Reporting Violations

If you encounter any content or behavior that violates our child safety standards, please report it immediately through:

  • The in-app report button on any user profile
  • Emailing us directly at hariomchaudharybusiness@gmail.com

All reports are reviewed promptly and taken seriously.

⚖️

Legal Compliance

Crushr complies with all applicable laws regarding the protection of minors, including but not limited to the Information Technology Act, 2000 (India) and relevant CSAM prevention regulations. We cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies in investigations related to child safety.

Child Safety Contact

For child safety concerns, CSAM reports, or compliance inquiries, contact our designated safety officer directly.
