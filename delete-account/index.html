Delete Account

Last updated: April 06, 2026

This page explains how you can permanently delete your Crushr account and all associated personal data from our systems.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account at any time directly from within the Crushr app. Please follow the steps below:

  1. Open the Crushr app on your device.
  2. Make sure you are signed in to the account you wish to delete.
  3. Tap the Profile icon in the bottom navigation bar.
  4. Scroll down and tap Settings.
  5. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  6. A confirmation dialog will appear — tap Delete to permanently remove your account.

Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once you delete your account, all your data — including your profile, photos, matches, and messages — will be permanently removed from our servers.

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, date of birth, bio, interests, gender preferences)
  • All profile photos uploaded to Crushr
  • Your swipe history (likes, dislikes, super likes)
  • All matches and conversations
  • Any reports or blocks associated with your account
  • Your authentication credentials (email and password)

All personal data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days of account deletion.
