Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective date: April 30, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 30, 2026  ·  Applies to: Crushr Premium & Gold subscriptions
Summary: Crushr subscriptions are billed monthly and are non-refundable once a billing cycle starts. You may cancel anytime — your access continues until the period ends. Refunds are issued only for payment errors, within 5–7 business days of a valid request.

1. Subscription Plans

2. Cancellation Policy

  • You may cancel your subscription at any time directly from within the Crushr app under Profile → Subscription → Cancel Plan.
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You will retain full access to your subscribed features until that date.
  • No further charges will be made after cancellation is confirmed.
  • Cancelling does not automatically trigger a refund for the current billing period.

3. Refund Policy

All subscription payments are non-refundable once a billing cycle has commenced, except in the following circumstances:

  • Duplicate charge: If you were charged more than once for the same billing period due to a technical error, a full refund of the duplicate amount will be issued.
  • Technical failure: If a payment was successfully deducted but your subscription was not activated due to a verified technical failure on our end, a full refund will be issued.
  • Unauthorized transaction: If a payment was made without your knowledge or consent and you report it promptly, we will review and process a refund if the claim is valid.

Refund requests must be raised within 7 days of the charge date to be eligible. Requests submitted after 7 days will not be considered.

Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days and credited to the original payment method used at the time of purchase.

No partial refunds are issued for unused days remaining in a billing cycle.
